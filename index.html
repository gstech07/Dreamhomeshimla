About Dream Homestay

  • Wake up to breathtaking cedar forests and valley sunrises.
  • Orchard-fringed gardens perfect for yoga, meditation, and evening tea.
  • Delight in warm, home-cooked Himachali food using garden herbs.
  • Bright, designer interiors with hand-painted art and mountain-inspired décor.
  • Enjoy true Himachali hospitality from a family deeply rooted in Shimla’s traditions.
  • Easy access to Mall Road, Ridge, Jakhoo Temple, Kufri, and local gems off the tourist track.
